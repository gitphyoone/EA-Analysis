
V19 FX Proprietary Desk Development Roadmap

Phase 1 – Core Signal Engine
1. Build PostgreSQL database:
   - market_data
   - trades
   - trade_history
   - quant_journal

2. Build MT4 Data Collector:
   - Collect H1 candle data
   - EMA50, EMA200, RSI14, ADX14, ATR14
   - Send data to FastAPI

3. Build Rule-Based Signal Engine:
   BUY:
   - EMA50 > EMA200
   - RSI > 55
   - ADX > 25

   SELL:
   - EMA50 < EMA200
   - RSI < 45
   - ADX > 25

   Otherwise:
   - NO_TRADE

Phase 2 – Risk Engine

1. Risk per trade:
   - 0.25% of account equity

2. Stop Loss:
   - ATR × 1.5

3. Take Profit:
   - ATR × 3.0

4. Lot Size Formula:
   Lots = Risk Amount / (Stop Loss Pips × Pip Value)

5. Circuit Breakers:
   - Daily DD = 3%
   - Weekly DD = 6%
   - Monthly DD = 10%

Phase 3 – Portfolio Engine

1. Maximum open positions:
   - 3 trades

2. Currency exposure basket:
   - USD
   - EUR
   - GBP
   - JPY
   - AUD
   - CAD

3. Exposure limit:
   - Absolute exposure <= Equity × 2

4. Correlation filter:
   - Calculate rolling correlation
   - Reject new trade if correlation > 0.80

Phase 4 – News Engine

1. Connect economic calendar API:
   - Forex Factory
   - MyFXBook
   - TradingEconomics

2. Store:
   - Event
   - Currency
   - Impact
   - Time

3. Trading restrictions:
   - No trading 30 minutes before red news
   - No trading 30 minutes after red news

Phase 5 – Session Engine

Trade only during:
- London Session
- New York Session
- London/New York Overlap

Avoid low-liquidity periods whenever possible.

Phase 6 – Trade Management

1. Entry executed

2. At +1R:
   - Move Stop Loss to Break-Even

3. At +2R:
   - Close 50% position

4. Remaining position:
   - ATR trailing stop

Phase 7 – Journal Engine

Store every closed trade:

- Ticket
- Symbol
- Entry Price
- Exit Price
- Stop Loss
- Take Profit
- Spread
- Commission
- Swap
- Net PnL
- R-Multiple

Phase 8 – Analytics Dashboard

Dashboard:
- Equity Curve
- Open Positions
- Daily Performance

Performance:
- Win Rate
- Profit Factor
- Sharpe Ratio
- Expectancy

Risk:
- Daily Drawdown
- Weekly Drawdown
- Monthly Drawdown

Trade History:
- Full trade records

Phase 9 – Validation

1. Walk-Forward Analysis
2. Out-of-Sample Testing
3. Forward Demo Testing (minimum 3 months)
4. Small Live Account ($500–$2,000)
5. Gradual Scaling

Final Architecture

MT4 / MT5
    ↓
FastAPI
    ↓
Signal Engine
    ↓
Risk Engine
    ↓
Portfolio Engine
    ↓
News Engine
    ↓
Execution Engine
    ↓
Journal Engine
    ↓

    ↓
Dashboard

Goal:
Build a stable, risk-controlled FX proprietary trading system suitable for personal capital, managed accounts, and future prop-desk expansion.
